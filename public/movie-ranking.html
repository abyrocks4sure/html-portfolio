The Best Movies According to Abhishek

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Matrix

This is my favourite sci-fi. I love the modern special effects and the storyline of the movie. It initially freed my mind.

A Beautiful Mind

Really cool biographical movie which goes on to show the power of perseverance.

The Pursuit of Happyness

Super beautiful film. Really moved me. there is no alternative to sacrifice in one's life.
